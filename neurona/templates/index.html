    Features

    Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

    From clear daily spending insights to long-term forecasts, Neurona gives you the tools to make informed financial decisions.

    🧠
    Personal AI Advisor

    AI analyzes your habits and suggests concrete optimizations, not generic tips. It learns from your behavior over time.

    📊
    Real-time dashboard

    View income, expenses, and balance with interactive charts. Every datum is updated in real-time and accessible from any device.

    🔒
    Privacy by design

    Your data remains yours. You can fully self-host or use our infrastructure — end-to-end encrypted, never sold to third parties.

    🎯
    Smart goals

    Set financial targets and let AI calculate the optimal path to reach them, adjusting the plan based on your income changes.

    🔗
    Import from anywhere

    CSV, OFX, QIF, Open Banking APIs: Neurona adapts to your data sources. No vendor lock-in, no dependency.

    ⚡
    Automations & rules

    Create custom rules to auto-categorize transactions, send alerts, or export reports. Finance that works for you.

    How it works

    Operational in under ten minutes

    01

    03

    Neurona automatically categorizes, detects patterns, and starts building your personal financial profile.

    Receive actionable insights

    Not just statistics: practical tips, proactive alerts, and forecasts on where your money is going.

    neurona / docker-compose.yaml
    # Self-host Neurona in 3 commands
     
    version: '3.9'
    services:
      neurona:
        image: ghcr.io/neurona/app:latest
        ports: ["3000:3000"]
        environment:
          AI_PROVIDER: local
          ENCRYPTION: true
     
    # Then:
    $ docker compose up -d
    # ✓ Neurona running at localhost:3000
